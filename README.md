# Robust_Gear

Robust Gear MotoWorks is a customized bike manufacturing company. This application enables their customers to customize the bike according to their required specifications. The customers can select from a wide variety of spares and additional gears such as engine, visor, touring accessories etc. They can also customize the color, performance assists and other designs with add on. The customer can preview the final product after submitting the list of customization options and the product will be delivered based on the availability & production time.
